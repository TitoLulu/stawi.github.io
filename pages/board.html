Board Of Directors

Sangu Delle - Chairman

Sangu Delle is a TED Global Fellow and the Founder and CEO of Golden Palm Investment(Investment holding company focussed on early stage opportunities in Africa). He has featured in Forbes' Africa Top 30 under 30 (2015) and Future Awards Africa 2014 "Young Person of the Year". Sangu, who received his eduaction at Harvard Business School, has worked previously at Valiant Capital Partners, Morgan Stanley and Goldman Sachs.

Fukunda Mbaru

Fukunda is an Investment banker at J.P Morgan South Africa and Nigeria. His previous employment has seen him work at Centum Investment Company Ltd and Morgan Stanely.

Daniel Obaseki

Daniel Obaseki is a Sub-Saharan Africa-focussed investor with experience across public and private equity platforms. He has worked in the past at Black River Asset Management, The Abraaj Group and Knife Capital.

Amanda Cotterman

Amanda is an Africa consumer business investor who owns a Kenyan daycare company. She has previously worked at Harvard Business School, McKinsey & Company, and Morgan Stanley.

Jennifer White

Jennifer is a Sub-Saharan Africa focussed investment manager based in Nairobi. She has worked previously at Kuramo Capital Management, Monitor Deloitte (ex Monitor Group), and Citi.

Eric Muthomi - CEO

Eric is a Mandela Washington Fellow (2014).He has featured in Forbes Africa Top 30 under 30 and Business Daily Top 40 under 40. Eric holds a bachelors degree in Law from Catholic University and a Global Executive MBA from United States International University
