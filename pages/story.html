Our Story

Stawi Foods is a Kenyan based agro processing company specializing in the processing of dried fruit and grains into nutritious flour blends that are pre-cooked and fortified.The company was founded by Eric Muthomin in 2012 with its premier products targeting childern and entire households and with an input material supply base from small holder producers. The company's leading local brands are Stawi Junior Porridge and Stawi Family Porridge, which are primarily being distributed through mainstream retail outlets across major Nairobi suburbs and Neighbouring counties

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading provider of nutritious processed foods in Kenya and a meaningful direct and indirect employer in Kenya.

Our Beliefs

We've been on a mission since day one: to provide better food for more people. We believe that access to nutritious delicious foods made with only natural ingredients is a right, not a privilege. We believe every food maker has a responsibility to provide people with better options, which is why we're so proud of the way our food is made.
