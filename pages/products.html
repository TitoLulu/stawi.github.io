Stawi Junior Porridge

This creamy precooked children's porridge is a tasty and nutritious meal for toddlers aged 6 months and above. Stawi Junior Porridge is prefectly blended with wimbi (millet), maize, fat-free soya, wheat, milk powder and essential vitamins. As porridge is considered gluten it should only be introduced to babies from 6 months and after consulting with your Padiatrician.

Benefits of Stawi Junior Porridge

T
  • Wimbi(millet)- is an easily digestible, non-glutinous and nutritious meal. It is rich in Vitamin B, phosphorous, Potassium, magnesium, and iron
  • Maize- source of protein, vitaminB1,B5, and C, folate and dietary fiber
  • Fat-free soya - rich in calcium and is cholesterol free
  • wholegrain wheat - easy to digest; a source of fiber and energy
  • Milk powder - Excellent source of calcium, protein, Vitamin A, B12, and D

Stawi Nurture Family
